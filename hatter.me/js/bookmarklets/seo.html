Search Engine Optimization Bookmarklets

These bookmarklets may be helpful in analyzing search engine positions, in combination with search bookmarklets. If you find these useful, you might also like the log analysis bookmarklets.

Customize the "find links to" bookmarklet to find links to:

(To keep bookmarklets in order to use them on other web pages, drag them to your Bookmarks Toolbar. Or, install them all at once.)

Bookmarklet Description Works in
find links to squarefree Selects a link to squarefree each time you click it. Customize above. Details
google backlinks Searches Google for PR4+ pages that link to the current PR4+ page. Details
atw internal backlinks Searches Alltheweb for backlinks from other pages on the site. Details
atw external backlinks Searches Alltheweb for backlinks from other sites. Details
atw plaintext backlinks Searches Alltheweb for non-hyperlinked "backlinks". Details
google site search: all Searches Google for all pages on the site. Details
atw site search: all Searches Alltheweb for all pages from the site. Details
number google hits Adds result numbers to Google SERPs, taking start= into account. Details
open in ie Opens the page in IE. After clicking, select "Open With... iexplore.exe". Details
word frequency Lists the words in the page and how many times each word appears. Details

Browsers: is Internet Explorer for Windows, is Netscape 7 or Mozilla, is Netscape 4, and is Opera.

I don't know if this is the best search to use. For my site, ATW's URL Inspector links to the search +url.all:www.squarefree.com +site:squarefree.com. I don't know how it computes the domain. or why removing the www makes a difference when all indexed pages have the www. Removing the +'s, removing one of the two "constraints", removing url.all's www, or adding www to site all make fewer sites appear, which is weird.
